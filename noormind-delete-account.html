Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your NoorMind account and all associated personal data by sending us an email. We will process your request within 30 days.

To request account deletion, please email us with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your NoorMind account.

📧 Request Account Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your name and email address
  • Account credentials and login information
  • Saved favourites and collections
  • Personalisation preferences and settings
  • Notification preferences

Data We May Retain

Some data may be retained for a limited period after deletion for legal and security purposes:

  • Transaction records — retained for up to 3 years as required by French law
  • Backup data — may persist for up to 90 days before being fully purged
⚠️ Please note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Your Premium subscription will not be automatically refunded. Please cancel your subscription through the App Store or Google Play before requesting account deletion.
